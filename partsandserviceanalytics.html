Parts and Service Analytics

Unlock new business opportunities

Automotive and Industrial manufacturers possess and manage very large OEM spare parts and service portfolios. To maintain position in a fast-changing and disruptive environment, the aftermarket parts and service managers need comprehensive and high-quality data that allow for fact-based and quick strategic or operative decision making. The EPARTS platform helps them find and display those data by means of modern data processing and interactive graphical visualizations.

From up-selling and service agreements suggestions to spare part and service demand forecasts, EPARTS can open up a brand new set of business opportunities.
