People

Be the next Key person.
We called ourselves Key Technologies because every technology has a key - and that key is people.

Are you a digital enthusiast?
Do you like the idea of facing cutting-edge technological challenges every day?

Here at Key Technologies, we give you a space to express your potential in a dynamic and flexible environment, that combines high technological value with a deep understanding of our customers’ needs and processes. You’ll find no routine here: we’re a lean structure with an informal attitude that encourages professional growth, with continuous training and defined professional careers.

If you are passionate and competent, you have good relationship skills too, and outstanding team spirit skills – then you’re ready to become one of our next Key persons.

We look for brilliant high school or university graduates in technical subjects, preferably information technology or computer engineering. You will speak a fluent English and will have gained some years of experience as developers in IT consultancy firms or software development companies, both B2B and B2C. We’re looking for people who are customer-oriented, willing to work by objectives, independent yet able to work in a team to complete shared projects.
