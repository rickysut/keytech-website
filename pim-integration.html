PRODUCT INFORMATION ONBOARDING FROM SUPPLIERS

You’re often onboarding thousands of products from thousands of suppliers. You may be dealing with seasonal launches, too.
Customers expectations for product information are changing, so don’t be limited by the data that is provided to you.
PIM Onboarding removes the dependency on your suppliers and allows you to scalably create workflows to onboard product data from those suppliers and manufacturers.

The seamless way to control the collection of product information from suppliers and manufacturers

DEFINE THE DATA ATTRIBUTES YOU NEED

Define the product information that your suppliers are responsible for providing to your team from directly within PIM. Get precisely the information you need from your suppliers to provide a great experience for your customers.

ASSIGN PRODUCTS TO SUPPLIERS

Once the product has been assigned to the supplier within PIM, the supplier is responsible for the required fields. Stop filtering through unnecessary information and huge spreadsheets, by only asking them for the data from the products that you need.

SUPPLIERS PROVIDE PRODUCT INFORMATION

Thanks to a secure and intuitive SaaS-based portal, all of your suppliers can submit precisely the product information you require in their own secure and branded portal.

REVIEW AND APPROVE

Feel confident in the quality of the data you’re receiving. Review and approve, or reject the submitted product information within an easy workflow, before letting messy data into the PIM

SIMPLIFY THE DATA SHARING PROCESS FROM YOUR PIM

Stop exporting product information, manipulating spreadsheets and exporting media assets. Instead, User can create a portal for secure, self-service, data sharing. Creating a Shared Catalog is easy.

Select the product information you want to share, any associated media types, like images, videos or pdfs, and then enter the email address of the recipient. Sharing product information with internal teams and retailers has never been so easy.

WHAT YOUR RECIPIENTS NEED WHEN THEY NEED IT

With Shared Catalogs, recipients of product data no longer need to wait to access the information that they need to do their job. Simply log in to the Shared Catalog, search and filter for the products they need, and voila!

Plus, makes it easy to export the desired product selection and you can always feel confident that the data being accessed is up-to-date, with syncs every ten minutes.
